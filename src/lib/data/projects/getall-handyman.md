<p><strong>GetAll</strong> was an on-demand services platform I worked on, covering mobile apps, a web dashboard, and backend infrastructure. The goal was to make it easier for users to find and book services, while giving providers proper tools to manage their work.</p><p>I worked across the customer apps (iOS and Android), the provider and admin dashboards, and the backend. A big focus was performance and usability. The existing setup worked, but it needed to feel faster, cleaner, and more reliable.</p><p>On the mobile side, I improved responsiveness, navigation, and overall UI flow. Users could browse services more easily, view provider profiles, get booking updates in real time, and receive notifications without friction.</p><p>For service providers, I refined the web dashboard to make day-to-day operations simpler. That included better availability management, clearer booking flows, built-in messaging, and basic analytics to help providers understand how their business was doing.</p><p>Behind the scenes, I optimized the backend using Firebase Cloud Functions. This covered data handling, scalability, and authentication, making sure the platform could grow without falling apart under load.</p><p>The project ultimately didn’t go live due to external challenges, but the core systems were solid. The work laid down a strong foundation across mobile, web, and backend, and it was a good example of building a full on-demand platform end to end, even when the final outcome depends on more than just code.</p><p><strong>Codebases:</strong></p><ul><li><a href="https://github.com/noelzappy/getall_customerapp?ref=noelzappy.dev">Customer App</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/noelzappy/getall_provider?ref=noelzappy.dev">Service Provider App</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/noelzappy/getall-website?ref=noelzappy.dev">Main Website</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/noelzappy/getall_admin?ref=noelzappy.dev">Admin Panel</a></li></ul>
