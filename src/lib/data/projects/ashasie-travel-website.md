<p><strong>Ashasie Travel &amp; Tours</strong> hired me to rebuild their website and give their online presence a much-needed refresh. The old site didn’t really reflect their brand or make it easy for customers to explore trips and book confidently.</p><p>The goal was straightforward: create a modern, easy-to-use website that clearly shows their travel packages and makes booking simple, whether you’re on a phone or a laptop.</p><p>I redesigned the site with a clean, responsive layout and focused on clarity over clutter. Visitors can browse tours easily, and the booking flow feels smooth and direct. I also integrated a secure booking system and user portal to improve how customers interact with the business.</p><p>On top of that, the site was structured with SEO in mind, helping Ashasie Travel show up better in search and attract more organic traffic.</p><p>The end result is a simple but polished website that fits the brand, works well on all devices, and makes it easier for customers to discover and book trips.
</p>
