<p><strong>Built.Africa</strong> is the official website for Built Financial Technologies, and I worked on it as part of my time collaborating with the team. The goal was to present Built clearly: who they are, what they do, and why they matter to SMEs across Africa.</p><p>The site needed to feel professional without being stiff. Fast, simple, mobile-friendly, and trustworthy were the priorities. It had to explain Built’s services clearly and make it easy for potential clients to reach out.</p><p>I worked on key parts of the frontend using React and Next.js. My focus was on responsive layouts, clean content structure, and performance. I helped shape sections that explain Built’s services, built interactive elements like the team profiles and contact forms, and made sure the site worked smoothly across devices.</p><p>Under the hood, I also paid attention to SEO, accessibility, and load times, so the site isn’t just nice to look at, but actually effective.</p><p>Today, the website acts as Built’s digital front door. It tells their story, builds trust, and helps connect them with businesses looking for practical financial and technology support.</p><p><strong>Tech:</strong> React, Next.js</p>
